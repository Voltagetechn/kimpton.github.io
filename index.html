About Us

At Kimpton Hotels, we believe in redefining the travel experience through boutique luxury, warm hospitality, and inspired design. With locations across the United States, each of our hotels offers a unique vibe that reflects the spirit of its city—while delivering the consistent comfort, service, and style our guests know and love. From the sunny coasts of California to the bustling streets of New York, Kimpton properties are thoughtfully designed to create memorable stays—whether you're traveling for business, pleasure, or a little of both. At every destination, we offer modern amenities, locally inspired details, and genuine connections that make every stay personal. Kimpton Hotel Wilshire in Los Angeles is just one example of how we bring luxury and local flavor together. Wherever you stay with us, you're part of something special.
